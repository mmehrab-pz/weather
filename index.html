    How to Use the Weather App

    Type a city name in the search bar to get detailed weather information. Here’s how it works:

    • Enter any city name (e.g. London, New York, Tehran).
    • Use English for city names to ensure accurate results.
    • The app provides full weather details for day and night, including:
      • Temperature
      • Wind speed
      • Humidity
      • Air pressure
      • Visibility
      • Overall weather condition
    • View all data on interactive charts for better visualization.
    • See the selected city on an interactive map.
    • From Settings, switch measurement units (°C / °F, km/h / mph).
    💡 Tip: Type city + country for better accuracy (e.g. Springfield, US).
    ⚠️ Note: Live data requires an internet connection and may vary slightly by source.
    Need help? Click the Help icon or contact support.
